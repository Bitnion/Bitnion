Bitnion (BNO) – A Fair and Foundational Cryptocurrency

Launched: June 2025
Developer: Bitnion Core Team (Anonymous)
Contact: bitnion@gmail.com

🔍 Overview

Bitnion is an independent cryptocurrency inspired by Bitcoin, offering similar technical guarantees while ensuring fair, non-commercial distribution.

Core Parameters (Defined in Code)

  • Name: Bitnion
  • Symbol: BNO
  • Smallest Unit: nion (1 BNO = 100,000,000 nion)
  • Total Supply: 15,000,000 BNO
  • Premine: 1,000,000 BNO in Genesis Block
  • Halving: Every 205,000 blocks
  • Estimated Final Mining: Year ~2137
  • Network ID: "bitnion"
  • Consensus: Bitcoin-compatible PoW (SHA-256)

📦 Premine Distribution Policy

1,000,000 BNO were premined at genesis for the following purposes:

  • 950,000 BNO – to be distributed fairly to early supporters who complete KYC via bitnion@gmail.com.
  • 50,000 BNO – reserved as symbolic reward for the development team (1 year of work).
  • Distribution is managed via 10 secure Bitnion Core wallets.

Eligibility

  • ✔️ 1 BNO for each KYC-verified wallet (free)
  • ✔️ +1 BNO bonus for verified miners who mine at least 5 BNO
  • ❗ Program ends when premine supply drops below 50,000 BNO

💝 Optional Community Support

Supporters may optionally donate via platforms like Tremendous. As a symbolic thank-you, the team may gift:

  • 5 BNO for $10–20 support
  • 10 BNO for $30–40 support

This is not a token sale or investment. Gifts are symbolic and subject to availability.

🛡️ Security & Ethics

Bitnion Core Team:

  • 🚫 Does not sell BNO under any condition
  • 🚫 Does not run any ICO, staking, or investment program
  • ✅ Supports responsible disclosure (see SECURITY.md)
  • ✅ Accepts contributions from public and anonymous developers

📈 On Exchange Listings

Bitnion is not listed on any exchange by default. If community-led efforts lead to listing, the Bitnion Core Team supports such initiatives provided they remain transparent and legal.
